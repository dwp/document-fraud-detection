Sign in to the Document Fraud Detection Service

This service checks if unexpected changes have been made to a document's pages (visible content) or the file's properties (hidden metadata). It suggest areas to examine during your review.

The service does not:

  • understand what the words or images on the pages mean
  • compare against other documents
  • indicate conclusively that fraud is present

If you feel further investigation is needed, you may include the information provided to support your referral.

Some examples of documents you can upload:

  • passports
  • birth certificates
  • utility bills
  • tenancy agreements
  • wage slips
  • bank statements
  • council tax documents
  • NHS letters
  • school letters

We currently only accept PDF format under 20MB.
