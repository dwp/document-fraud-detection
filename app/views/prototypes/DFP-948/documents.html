What is a document check?

We check each document against a set of criteria to detect possible document tampering in the document's content (visible) or data (hidden). These checks suggest further areas to examine within your own review.

These checks do not:

  • understand what the content in the document contextually means
  • assume relationships between other documents
  • indicate conclusively that fraud is present

If you determine further investigation is needed by a decision maker or investigations team, you may include the information provided in these checks to support your review.

You can upload:

  • passports
  • birth certificates
  • utility bills
  • tenancy agreements
  • wage slips
  • bank statements
  • council tax documents
  • NHS letters
  • school letters
