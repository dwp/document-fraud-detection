How to confirm the checks

Concealed text or covered content

Select all information in the document to highlight any empty selections that may be hiding text in important areas, such as the address or amounts of money.

Document quality

Zoom in around areas that appear blurred, jagged or off-colour in comparison to the rest of the document. This may be a sign that changes were made or that the document was created from different sources.

Text alignment, size or spacing

Compare how letters, numbers and words line and size up to each other. Anything that seems out of place may be a sign that something was replaced or removed.

Passport code

Send the document to the Document Examination Team for further examination.

Unexpected characters

Select all document content and copy it into a text editor like Microsoft Word.

If it is mostly symbols with a few recognisable words in important places, like names, address or transactions, it may be a sign that changes were made.

If you are only able to copy certain words, this may be a sign that they were added later.

Untrusted branding

Zoom in to search for a second logo or brand that should not be there. For example, a utility bill should not have branding for a freelance or template website.
